Invoice Capture

Eliminate the hassle of paper invoices and speed up the accounts payable process.

AP teams waste a lot of time sorting, scanning, and entering data from paper invoices. With Invoice Capture, your organization can eliminate tedious data entry, improve accuracy, and accelerate invoice turnaround times. Vendors send invoices to Concur Invoice either by email, mail or electronically Data like vendor name, invoice number, date, PO number, and more is captured using OCR technology Captured invoice data is validated by SAP Concur The invoice is routed for approval and payment
